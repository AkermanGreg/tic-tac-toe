Tic Tac Toe is a two player game that can be played on either one or two computers, smartphones, or tablets.

X always moves first and places a piece on any empty space. Then it's O's turn to do the same. A player wins by being the first to connect three spaces in a row. If no player is able to connect three spaces, it's a tie and the game is over. Enjoy!
